wudu (ablution)

Wudu is the Islamic act of purification and ablution. It is a step-by-step process that not only physically purifies us but also spiritually prepares us for the act of prayer.

Steps to perform ablution (wudu)

1.Intention (Niyyah)

2.Wash your hands

3.Rinse nose and mouth

4.Wash your face

5.Wash your arms

6.Wipe your head

7.Clean your ears

8.Wash your feet
