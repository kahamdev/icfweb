Salat  (prayer)

Salah (Prayer) is the second pillar of Islam.It was the first act of worship that was made obligatory by Allah u and it is the last thing to be taken away from the religion. When it perishes, Islam will perish.

prayer times

1.FAJR(Dawn Prayer): Fajr is performed after dawn and before sunrise. Fajr consists of 2 Units (raka’ah).

2.DHUHR(Noon Prayer): Dhuhr is performed when the sun passes the meridian. Dhuhr consists of 4 Units (raka’ah).

3.ASR(Afternoon Prayer): Asr is performed midway between noon and sunset. Asr consists of 4 Units (raka’ah).

4.MAGHREB(Sunset Prayer): Maghreb is performed immediately after sunset. Maghreb consists of 3 Units (raka’ah).

5.ISHA(Night Prayer): Isha is performed after twilight up until fajr although it is preferred to be prayed before midnight. Isha consists of 4 units (raka’ah).
