Was islam spread by the sword?

The answer is No.

There is No Compulsion in Religion, “Let there be no compulsion in religion, for the truth stands out clearly from falsehood” (2:256). Ismāʿīl ibn Kathīr (d. 1373), said that this passage means, “Do not force anyone to become Muslim, for Islam is plain and clear, and its proofs and evidence are plain and clear. Therefore, there is no need to force anyone to embrace Islam.”
